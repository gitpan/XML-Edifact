4. Known Bugs

4.1 Double namespace declarations

Namespace declaration was redefined in January 1999. XML::Edifact produces both the old and the new declarations. You may have to edit XML::Edifact search for HERE_ and adopt the headers to your browsers preferences. XML::Edifact 0.4 will drop the old declartions!

4.2 Stating level in Syntax identifier.

This has to be parsed. The stating level in EDIFACT speak is called charset encoding normaly, and its of course important if you thing about non US/UK products. See un_edifact/unsl.
